Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

1 cup broccoli florets
1 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, or green)
1 cup sliced carrots
1/2 cup snap peas
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp grated ginger
Salt and pepper, to taste
Sesame seeds (for garnish)
Instructions:

Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
Add broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
Add snap peas and continue to stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
Pour in soy sauce, season with salt and pepper. Toss well to coat all vegetables.
Cook for another minute until vegetables are crisp-tender.
Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot, alone or with rice/noodles.
